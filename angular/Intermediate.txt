1️⃣ What is a Module in Angular?

A module (@NgModule) groups components, directives, pipes, and services into a logical unit.
Example: AppModule, SharedModule, AuthModule.


2️⃣ What are Lifecycle Hooks?

Lifecycle hooks allow running logic at specific moments of a component’s life.
Important hooks:

ngOnInit()

ngOnChanges()

ngAfterViewInit()

ngOnDestroy()

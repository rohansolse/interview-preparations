| Method    | Purpose        | Body | Idempotent | Usage                   |
| --------- | -------------- | ---- | ---------- | ----------------------- |
| **GET**   | Fetch data     | ❌    | ✔          | Read-only operations    |
| **POST**  | Create data    | ✔    | ❌          | Register user, add item |
| **PUT**   | Full update    | ✔    | ✔          | Replace entire record   |
| **PATCH** | Partial update | ✔    | ✔          | Update only one field   |
